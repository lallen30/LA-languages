Help & Guide Help & Guide Spaced Repetition System

This app uses a scientifically-proven spaced repetition algorithm to help you learn more effectively by showing cards at optimal intervals.

How It Works:

  • New cards appear immediately for learning
  • Easy cards reappear after longer intervals (days/weeks)
  • Good cards reappear at standard intervals
  • Hard cards reappear sooner for more practice
  • "I don't know" cards reset to day 1 and need relearning
The algorithm automatically adjusts based on your performance to optimize long-term retention. Study Session Guide

During Study Sessions:

Easy You knew the answer immediately and confidently
Good You knew the answer after thinking briefly
Hard You struggled but eventually remembered
I don't know You couldn't remember or got it wrong

Intra-Session Repetition:

Cards marked as Hard or I don't know will reappear within the same study session for immediate reinforcement:

  • Hard cards reappear after ~8 cards for additional practice
  • "I don't know" cards reappear after ~3 cards for quick review
 Session Completion

When you finish a study session, you'll see a summary with three options:

Review Missed Cards Practice only the cards you marked "I don't know" for focused learning
Study More Cards Continue with more cards from the same deck
Finish Return to the Decks page
Settings & Customization

Max Cards Per Session:

Control how many cards you study in each session (5-500 cards). The app will show:

  • Due cards that are ready for review
  • New cards to fill up to your maximum
Tip: Start with 20 cards per session and adjust based on your available time and energy.

Other Customizations:

  • Auto-speak on card flip: Automatically pronounce words when you flip cards
  • Color themes: Customize button colors and appearance
  • Text-to-speech: Configure voice and language settings
 Tips & Best Practices
Study consistently: Daily 10-15 minute sessions are more effective than long, infrequent sessions.
Be honest with responses: Choose the difficulty that truly reflects your knowledge for optimal scheduling.
Use "Review Missed Cards": Immediately practice difficult cards for better retention.
Check your stats: Monitor progress and accuracy in the Stats tab to track improvement.
Use audio features: Enable auto-speak to improve pronunciation and listening skills.
Organize your decks: Create focused decks by topic or difficulty level for targeted learning.
Card Management

Managing Your Cards:

  • Manage Deck: View, edit, or delete individual cards
  • Reset Progress: Reset individual cards or entire decks to start fresh
  • Search Cards: Find specific cards within your decks

Card Types:

  • Picture-Word: Image with Spanish word and English translation
  • Fill-in-the-Blank: Complete sentences with missing words
  • Translation: Translate between languages in both directions
 Getting Started
  1. Create a deck or import existing cards
  2. Add cards using the + button on the Decks page
  3. Start studying by tapping the Study button
  4. Answer honestly using Easy, Good, Hard, or "I don't know"
  5. Review missed cards for immediate reinforcement
  6. Study daily to maintain momentum and see progress
Remember: Learning is a journey. Be patient with yourself and celebrate small victories!
